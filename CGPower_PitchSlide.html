02 — Why now
Industry Tailwinds & Growth Drivers
T&D Supercycle — ₹9 Trillion through 2032
India must add 300 GW renewables by 2030 + serve 400 GW peak demand (vs 243 GW today). Transformer lead times are 18–36 months. Domestic supply constrained → pricing power locked in. Mordor: India transformer market $3B→$4.8B at 8.2% CAGR to 2031.
Data Centre Boom → Premium Transformer Demand
India data centre capacity: 1.4 GW → 9 GW by 2030 (6x). Each GW needs ~2–3 GW transformer capacity. CG's ₹900 Cr US data centre export order proves global pricing power. Nomura explicitly names CG as top data centre infrastructure pick.
India Semiconductor Mission — OSAT Policy Moat
$10B ISM with 50% capex subsidy. Cabinet-approved Feb 2024. CG Semi is the first ISM OSAT facility to be inaugurated — 2–3 years ahead of any future competitor. Commercial chips targeted CY2026; MOFSL expects OSAT losses to narrow from FY28.
Order Book Momentum — 1.6x Book-to-Bill
FY26 order inflows: ₹19,616 Cr (+33%). Backlog: ₹17,107 Cr (+61%). Power Systems backlog alone up 91% YoY. Visibility for FY27/28 is the strongest in company history.

03 — Why CG wins
Moats
765kV Oligopoly — 2-Player Domestic Club
Only CG Power + BHEL manufacture 765kV transformers at commercial scale in India. Takes 10–15 yrs to build: engineering know-how, HV test labs, PGCIL qualification. Not replicable in <5 years by any new entrant.
Capacity Expansion — Ahead of the Supply Curve
Transformer capacity expanding to 110 GVA (+1.5x) by 2026. Greenfield switchgear facility approved (₹748 Cr, doubles capacity by FY28). In a supply-constrained market, faster delivery = pricing power.
CG Semi — First-Mover + Renesas Anchor
ISM-backed, G1 operational, G2 on track. Any new Indian OSAT entrant today would be 2–3 yrs behind on facility + 12–24 months behind on customer qualification. Renesas partnership provides immediate customer pipeline — no competitor can replicate this.
Murugappa Governance Premium
From SFIO-investigated fraud (2019) to two successful QIPs (₹3,500 Cr in FY25 + ₹3,000 Cr in FY24) subscribed by global institutional investors. Governance transformation is complete — and priced in only partially.
